Episodes

Film nerds and hosts Caroline Young and Chris Masciarelli discuss their favorite films snubbed at the Oscars. Alternating each week, one host must convince the other of their favorite films’ deserved nomination. They discuss Oscar films and their competitive awards years, while also trying to make each other laugh.
