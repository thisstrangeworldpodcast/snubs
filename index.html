Episodes

Film nerds and hosts Caroline Young and Chris Masciarelli discuss their favorite films snubbed at the Oscars. Alternating each week, one host must convince the other of their favorite films’ deserved nomination. They discuss Oscar films and their competitive awards years, while also trying to make each other laugh.

  • Mar 17, 2022

    Zodiac - Season 1 Episode 4

    On Snubs, film fans and co-hosts, Caroline Young and Chris Masciarelli, discuss their favorite films that were snubbed by the Oscars. On this episode, our titular hosts discuss David Fincher’s Zodiac and the 2008 Academy Awards it was noticeably absent from. Caroline wants David Fincher to make serial killer movies until the end of time, and Chris probably wrote the Zodiac letters (but don’t tell anyone).
